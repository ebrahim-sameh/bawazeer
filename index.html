Corporate History

  • The company was established in 1960 A.D., which means it has been operating for about 46 years. We specialize in importing and marketing a wide range of products, including medicines, pharmaceutical products, herbal items, medical rehabilitation equipment, optical frames, lenses, baby care products, and cosmetics, among others.
  • Our company is one of the oldest trading houses in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, known for its strength and reliability in this field. We prioritize enhancing our services to meet the stringent standards set by the Ministry of Health, Ministry of Commerce and Industry, and other medical authorities. This dedication is made possible thanks to our qualified and skilled staff members, both in management and technical roles.
  • Through the relentless efforts of our dedicated staff and vigilant supervision across all departments, our company has achieved remarkable success in its field. We have received certificates of merit and trophies from the Saudi Ministries of Health and Commerce. Additionally, we've been honored with letters of appreciation from our clients and partner companies. Moreover, we proudly serve as a member of the Patients Welfare Committee in Makkah and as a member of the Medical Committee under the Chamber of Commerce and Industry, Makkah.

Corporate Mission and Vision:

  • At Bawazeer Group, our mission is to revolutionize pharmaceutical services in the Middle East.
  • We are committed to providing cutting-edge solutions and forging strategic partnerships that elevate the entire industry. Our vision is to be the leading force in advancing healthcare through innovation, research, and collaboration. We aim to empower patients, healthcare professionals, and the pharmaceutical sector at large, contributing to the betterment of health and well-being across the region.

Heritage and Partnerships:

  • Our heritage is deeply rooted in our commitment to excellence and our enduring partnerships. We are proud to collaborate with Apex Pharmacy Industries and operate a Bioequivalence company, bolstering the clinical trials ecosystem across the Middle East. Additionally, our ownership shares in esteemed pharmacy chains such as El-Ezaby Pharmacies in Egypt and the Bawazeer chain in Saudi Arabia reflect our enduring legacy of trust and quality.
  • Our wholesale company complements our portfolio by offering a wide range of top-tier pharmaceutical products, ensuring our customers have access to the best in the industry.

Services

Check our Services

Training

Our training programs offer comprehensive and up-to-date knowledge in the pharmaceutical field, focusing on industry trends, regulations, and best practices to enhance professional skills and capabilities.

Research

At the core of our mission, our research team engages in innovative projects that advance drug discovery and provide critical market insights, shaping the future of healthcare and pharmaceuticals.

Pharmacovigilance

Dedicated to patient safety, our Pharmacovigilance Center monitors and reports adverse drug reactions, working in collaboration with the pharmaceutical industry and regulatory authorities to ensure the effective use of medications.

Bioequivalence

Specializing in bioequivalence testing, our company conducts rigorous clinical trials to ensure generic pharmaceutical products meet the same standards as their branded counterparts, using state-of-the-art facilities and expert teams.

Biostatistics

Our Biostatistics service offers comprehensive statistical support for pharmaceutical and healthcare industries, ensuring research and clinical trials are backed by robust statistical analysis for accurate and reliable results.

Regulatory Consultation

Our Regulatory Consultation service provides expert guidance on local and international pharmaceutical regulations, assisting in product registrations, quality compliance, and strategic market access to ensure regulatory adherence and market success.
